## 1. What Information We Collect

When you create an account on our website, register for a course, or fill out a form to enquire about certain courses, you must submit credentials such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other pertinent data. The data is usually kept with us for one year for future reference.

Any information we collect from you is used in one of the following ways:

- To continuously work toward improving our website offerings based on the information and feedback we gather from you
- To improve our overall service so we can timely respond to your service requests and attend to all the support you need
- To process all financial and other transactions

Your information, whether private or public, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your prior consent, other than to ensure the service requested. However, the information may be used to send you notifications and updates pertaining to your query, as well as occasional communication such as company newsletters, updates, promotional information, and related product or service information.

## 3. How We Protect Your Information

When you make an online purchase, several security precautions are put in place to protect your information. We take necessary security measures to ensure that credentials are kept safe. Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology is used to transmit the data that our users share, thus protecting it from unauthorized access and maintaining confidentiality. For secure and convenient online transactions, we employ 256-bit Secure Sockets Layer Protocol (SSL) and additional safeguards.

## 4. Cookies

We use cookies. Cookies are tiny files that allow users to send any type of data. The relevant information is typically remembered and recognized by the browser. Cookies' primary function is to assist customers in remembering and understanding the products retained in their shopping carts. They integrate website traffic statistics, provide customers with a user-friendly experience, and keep them informed about the latest tools and other pertinent information.

## 5. Refund Policy

If participants wish to not proceed with the training after registration due to any reason, they are entitled to a refund of the fees. The refund will be initiated only if we are notified in writing within 2 days from the date of initial registration. The refund will be processed within 4 weeks from the day of withdrawal.
